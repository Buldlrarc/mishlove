THE BOOKS

every SOL in, every SOL out, one public wallet

When we petitioned for this CTO we expected the token's accrued creator rewards, roughly $18,000, to fund everything: the charity donations, the promotion, the development. Those rewards were claimed by the original creator 1 minute and 47 seconds before our takeover was approved. We started from zero.

Since then everything runs through one public wallet. Team members put in personal money, community members pitch in for specific pushes like DexScreener boosts, and creator fees accrue as trading happens. Every transaction is listed below with what it was for and a link to verify it on-chain.

On the charity percentage: we committed to donating from day one and made our first donation before we ever announced it. We have not locked a fixed percentage yet because the same small pot currently has to keep the chart alive and fund the donations. Locking a number before the fee flow stabilizes would either shortchange the rescue or starve the project that feeds it. When the CTO sets the split, it will be posted here first, with the reasoning.

No hidden wallets. No promises we can't keep. The log is the whole story.
